About Our Predictive Maintenance App

Welcome to Our App

Our predictive maintenance application is designed to revolutionize the way maintenance is performed. By leveraging advanced algorithms and data analytics, our app can predict equipment failures before they occur, saving time, money, and resources.

This application is a project developed by engineering students at Ferhat Abbas University. Our goal is to apply the knowledge we have gained during our studies to solve real-world problems and make a positive impact in the field of maintenance engineering.
